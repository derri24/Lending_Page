Today we will talk about why it is worth taking care of nature.

LET'S SAVE THE ENVIRONMENT TOGETHER

ELECTRICITY

Saving energy is an important way to reduce the environmental burden and reduce energy costs. The necessary measures include reassessing the use of household appliances, using light only when necessary, and isolating the house.

UNITY

Unity with nature is necessary for man, because the soul of man and the soul of nature are one soul. Only by understanding each other can we find balance.

WATER

Water is the source of life on Earth. It is necessary to reduce the amount of its consumption, prevent leakage, tighten the taps in time, do not pollute and use water resources rationally.

PLANTS

Plants need to be protected for the reason that they play an important role in the vital activity of all living organisms on Earth. Without them, existence on our planet would be impossible. Plants produce oxygen, which is needed for both animals and humans to breathe.

RECYCLE

The benefits of recycling are obvious. Thanks to it, fewer toxins are released into the atmosphere, the amount of harmful substances in the soil is reduced, and nothing threatens the lives of people and plants anymore.

ANIMALS

Animals need to be protected because animals can protect, also give us different products, wool, etc. But not only for its own benefit, but also for the sake of nature itself, because nature consists mainly of animals.

NATURE IS AMAZING

The emotion and delight that we experience from the contemplation of nature is a memory of the time when we were animals, trees, flowers, earth. More accurately: this is the consciousness of unity with everything, hidden from us by time.


WE ARE ENVIRONMENTAL VOLUNTEERS

Environmental volunteering is voluntary and gratuitous participation in work or activities aimed at solving environmental problems.

How to become an environmental volunteer?

Environmental volunteering is a personal decision that starts with your love for the environment. These environmental volunteer programs start with love and encourage you to do something in the immediate environment in which you are. In fact, we all have to be volunteers in one place or another.
